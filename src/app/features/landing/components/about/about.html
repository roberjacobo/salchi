About Dachshunds

Origin & History

Dachshunds originated in Germany over 300 years ago as badger hunters. Their distinctive long bodies were perfect for going underground.

Personality

Bold, curious, and loyal companions with big dog attitudes in small bodies. They form strong bonds with their families.

Key Traits

  • Intelligent and independent
  • Energetic and playful
  • Excellent watchdogs
  • 12-16 year lifespan
